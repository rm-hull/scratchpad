Privacy Policy

scratchpad is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy
Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard 
your personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to make it clear that scratchpad does not collect, process, or store
any personal data from its users. We do not gather information such as names,
addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally 
identifiable information. All data is stored soley in browser local storage, 
or if you choose, can be synchronised to your Google Drive account.

2. Third-Party Services

scratchpad utilizes third-party services (e.g Google Drive) for syncing data to
your Google account. These services are chosen carefully to ensure they align
with our commitment to user privacy. However, it's essential to review and
understand the privacy policies of these third-party services.

3. Security Measures

Although we do not collect personal data, we employ industry-standard security
measures to protect any non-personal information gathered during app usage.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

scratchpad reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any 
time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon 
posting the updated policy on our app.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please 
contact us at privacy.policy@destructuring-bind.org.

Thank you for using scratchpad.
